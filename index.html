    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

    What is an NFT ?

    NFT stands for Non-Fongible Token, unique digital assets that can be bought, traded and sold. They usually take the form of some type of artwork, or even in-game assets for certain virtual worlds. Each NFT is its own metadata codes, which are stored on the blockchain, to ensure that they remain unique. In that sense, it preserves the rarity of the digital asset at hand.

    Isn’t “mint” a chewing-gum flavor !?

    Yes it is, but not only ! In crypto, Mint or Minting refers to the phase when NFTs are published on the blockchain and become available for purchase on Marketplaces (OpenSea, Rarible, Zora). NFTs can only be purchased using Cryptocurrencies (well-known wallets include Coinbase, MetaMask, Rainbow).

    OpenSea ? I thought the travel was on a BitAnon NFT's, not a boat…

    You’re right, we’ll be flying, not sailing, but OpenSea doesn’t actually refer to the vast ocean in that case. It’s an online marketplace where you can buy, sell and store NFTs. You need a wallet holding cryptocurrencies to be linked to OpenSea to start trading.

    What are Gas Fees ? I thought BitAnon used Super Refined Potions

    To be honest, it’s the love of our community propelling us forward more than anything, but to answer the question : Gas is the computing power needed to validate a particular transaction on Ethereum. Gas fees are basically transaction costs.
